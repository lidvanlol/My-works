Sell

At Divine Diamond Cutters, we understand that selling your jewelry can be a stressful and time consuming process. We strive to make the process as easy as possible by offering a complimentary evaluation within minutes. There is no obligation to sell and each piece is evaluated in front of you in our showroom, through email or through phone consolation.

What We Buy

Diamonds & Gemstones


- We buy loose diamonds of any size, shape, make and quality.
- Vintage and antique diamonds are our specialty.
- Even broken or badly damaged diamonds have value.
- In addition to diamonds, we buy precious gemstones such as sapphire, emerald and ruby.

Jewelry


- We buy designer signed and non-signed fine jewelry.
- Even if your jewelry is broken or missing pieces, we can reuse or re-purpose anything of value.
-Antique and vintage jewelry is our specialty and we have the knowledge and understanding to fully assess your old world pieces.
- Please contact us if you are selling an estate.
