About us

Divine Diamond Cutters is headed by an original master of diamond cutting with over 40 years of experience. Ygal Yaakov began cutting diamonds in Tel Aviv, Israel under his uncle. The family is part of a deep heritage of diamond cutting that has been passed down over 100 years. Beginning in the 1970’s, Ygal worked as a production manager in Tel Aviv overseeing the factory’s diamond cutting production. He mastered the diamond cutting trade during this time by specializing in cutting rough diamonds into polished goods. In the 1980’s, Ygal began to travel the world to spread his knowledge as a diamond cutting master. He has spent over a decade teaching others in factories located in Bangkok, Thailand, Johannesburg, South Africa, Antwerp, Belgium, Moscow, Russia and Mumbai, India. In some factories, the production would be as great as 5000 stones per day.

In 1995, Ygal came to Chicago to pursue a better life. Since then, he has focused on repairing diamonds and improving their cut quality. He has worked on many important stones which are over 10 carats in size. Trusted by many within the trade community, Ygal developed a reputation as an expert with the skill to maximize light performance while also minimizing loss of carat weight. He is also an expert in enhancing the natural hue of fancy colored diamonds, having improved diamond color from fancy to fancy intense or fancy light to fancy designation as graded by GIA. Today, as the head diamond cutter for Divine Diamond Cutters, Ygal combines old world knowledge and experience with new world technology and efficiency. From simple repairs, to recutting to triple excellent, Divine Diamond Cutters is your reliable source to increase the value of your diamond. At no obligation, we offer a free consultation to repair or sell your diamonds or jewelry. Integrity and reputation is our utmost goal and relationships with our clients always come first.
